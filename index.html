Privacy Policy - VillaDent

Effective Date: 2025-08-21

VillaDent ("the App") is a dental clinic management application designed to help dentists manage patients, appointments, treatments, invoices, and X-ray records. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Information: Name, email, and login credentials (securely stored).
  • Patient Records: Patient phone numbers, notes, treatment details, appointment histories.
  • Medical Data: Uploaded X-ray images and associated case details.
  • Financial Data: Invoices and payment records generated by the clinic.

Note: The app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that track user behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To securely manage your app account and authentication (via JWT).
  • To organize and store patient data, treatments, and schedules.
  • To handle X-ray uploads and storage.
  • To generate and send invoices to clients.
  • To deliver appointment reminders and relevant notifications.

3. Data Storage & Security

X-ray images and sensitive records are securely stored using Microsoft Azure Blob Storage.

Authentication is protected using JWT tokens.

We employ encryption and industry best practices to ensure data security.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with unauthorized third parties.

Data access is limited to essential service providers (e.g., Microsoft Azure) strictly for app functionality.

5. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have full control over your data. You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and patient data.
  • Request corrections or updates to inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently.

Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all data associated with it (including patient records, appointments, X-rays, and invoices) directly from within the app's settings. This action is immediate and irreversible. Once confirmed, all your data will be permanently erased from our servers.

Please note: Deletion is subject to any legal obligations we might have to retain certain records for a mandated period.

6. Children’s Privacy

VillaDent is not designed for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy over time. Any updates will be clearly marked with a new “Effective Date” and communicated via the app.
